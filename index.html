Hassle-free registration of ownership using DLT technology

Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) is way to store information in tamper proof way with exact unchangable timestamp in popular distributed ledgers: bitbocin blockchain, ethereum blockchain or private blockchains (distributed ledgers).

First digital fingerprints is created from your image. Then they are stored on most popular blockchains: bitcoin blockchain, ethereum blockchain, and in our private database. Two fingerprints are created. First allows to identify your image uniquely and second is for searching for similar images. Once you have your unique image identifier, it is stored on blockchain together with your name and your digital signature. If you haven't digital signature, it is generated by us.

Once data is saved, it gets timestamped. And as blockchain is tamper-proof and could not be reversed or changed and also provides exact time when data was added to it as well as identifier of person who added, you can use this data as verifiable proof that in this moment of time this image with this unique id existed and was added by this author. This could be treated as publishing in well-known public source and proves your initial ownership over image based on precenence basis: first who published image in public is its owner.

After registration on blockchains you get certificate with all necessary data to prove your authorship which could be used even without our service

What will happen if someone will stole your photo and register it over this technology before you?
In depends on what other evidences you have. The same situation as if someone stole your photo and registered it in US copyright office as its own.
If your photo was never published anywhere, you'll need to use your other evidences of ownership - raw images with timestamp or unveil image published in some source.
If your photo was already published, our service (as well as US copyright office) will not regisgter it if photo could be found online.
In case you register your photo with our service but don't publish and someone steal it and register again, he'll got timestamp in later time. So as you have certificate with timestamp earlier in time, you're legal owner. But even more, we'll try our best not to allow registering duplicates over our service

What will happen if you sell image to someone so she is now legal owner of image and copyright owner
Its totally ok and normal situation. You still be treated as author and your certificate with blockchain-timestamped data will confirm it. While new owner will have its own document confirming his right for image. But you are still author of it. And new owner won't be able to register image using our service as "author" because you registered and have certificate with timestamp in past.

Automatic monitoring for photo appearance on major photostocks and other internet sites

Our service constantly runs checks using other well-known services like google image search as well as our own robot to check if any of your images appeared on some photo-sharing site, photostock or other site in internet

In case we found such image appearance, we notify you so you can decide if it is legitimate usage or not.

If its legitimate, we save it in our database and don't bother you again

In case it is not, you can file up a takedown notice to the site with request to remove infrigement and providing our certificate

We can help with creating such notice and now we're working on providing more legal support
